# Acme Widgets — Support Knowledge Base

## Refunds and returns

Our refund window is 30 days from the date of purchase. Physical goods must be
returned unused and in original packaging; we email a prepaid return label once
a return is approved. Digital goods are non-refundable once downloaded. Approved
refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days.

## Shipping

Standard shipping is 3–5 business days within the continental US and is free on
orders over 50 USD. Expedited (1–2 business day) shipping is available at
checkout. We currently ship to the US, Canada, and the UK. Tracking is emailed
when an order leaves our warehouse.

## Warranty

All Acme Widgets carry a 12-month limited warranty covering manufacturing
defects. The warranty does not cover accidental damage or normal wear. To make a
claim, contact support with your order id and a photo of the defect.

## Account and billing

Subscriptions renew automatically on the monthly anniversary of signup. You can
cancel any time from Account → Billing; cancellation stops the next renewal and
keeps access through the paid period. We do not prorate partial months.
